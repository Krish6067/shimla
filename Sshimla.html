Top 10 Tourist Places

1. The Ridge

The epicentre of all tourist activities in Shimla. Easily connected to Mall Road, Scandal Point, and Lakkar Bazaar. It hosts the famous Summer Festival.

Timing: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Cost: Free

Distance: 2.1 km from Bus Stand

2. The Mall Road

A vibrant lane for shopping, eateries, and cafes. No vehicles are allowed here, making it perfect for a leisurely stroll.

Timing: All Day

Cost: Free

Distance: 6 km from Bus Stand

3. Green Valley

A popular shooting spot for Bollywood movies, offering endless scenic beauty and lush greenery.

Timing: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Cost: Free

Location: Near Kufri

4. Christ Church

The second oldest church in North India, built in 1857. A majestic neo-gothic structure.

Timing: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Cost: Free

Location: The Ridge

5. Indian Institute of Advanced Study

Formerly the Viceregal Lodge, this historic building is a marvel of Victorian architecture.

Timing: Tue-Sun, 9AM - 5PM

Entry: ₹40 (Indians), ₹85 (Foreigners)

Note: Closed on Mondays

6. Himachal State Museum

Preserves the rich cultural heritage of the state with miniature paintings, sculptures, and coins.

Timing: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed Mon)

Entry: ₹10 (Indians), ₹50 (Foreigners)

7. Chadwick Falls

Nestled in Glen Forests, this waterfall cascades down 86 meters. Best visited during monsoons.

Timing: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Best time: June to September

8. Jakhoo Hill

Highest peak in Shimla (8000 ft) featuring a giant 108 ft Hanuman statue. Great for trekking.

Timing: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Activity: Trekking, Religious

9. Kufri

An adventure hub located 17km from Shimla. Famous for yak rides, skiing, and stunning views.

Timing: Open all day

Distance: 17 km from Shimla

10. Annandale

A flat terrain that served as a playground for the British. Houses an Army Museum and Golf Course.

Museum Timing: 10AM-2PM & 3PM-5PM

Distance: 4 km from Ridge
